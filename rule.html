Rules and Regulation

1. Civil engineer should have proper knowledge of different tests of building materials.
2. A civil engineer should plan for disaster instead of perfection.
3. A civil engineer should be responsible for every disasters.
4. Coordinate the pertinent devices needed for contractorsand workers to comply with the preventive principles andfollow the safety and health plan
5. Provide for modiﬁcations of the safety and health planwhen necessary.
6. Coordinate arrangements to check that the working pro-cedures are being implemented correctly.
7. Adopt the measures necessary to ensure that only authorized persons are allowed onto the construction site.


Civil Promotion criteria


Unlike other promotions, civil engineering promotions tend to occur slowly. There are several factors to consider here. The person who does well gets promoted faster while the one who the not so competent has to keep trying to get on next rank. If you’re working for a small local firm that focuses on surveying and residential development they may start letting you run smaller projects like septic field relocation even before you get your license. If however you’re at a larger company working on multiple million transportation or structural projects you should be aware that taking a project management role at that level takes a lot more experience beyond getting your license. Those jobs move much slower so it takes more time to see every phase of a job enough times to be ready to manage one.

Junior Engineer

The first level of engineering is Junior Engineer. In order to get to this level or rank, the person must be in service for six years and should persue bachelors of degree. They will be qualified for the next rank as Assistant Engineer.

Assistant Engineer Promotion

Assistant Engineer starts from P4. To get promoted to this rank, one must be in service of five years in Junior Engineer. Dergree certificate is needed in order to get to this level.

Deputy Executive Promotion

Deputy Executive Engineer is a P3 rank engineer and in order to get into next promotion or rank, he or she has to wait or work in this field for four years.

Executive Promotion

Deputy Executive Engineer is the promoted to Executive Engineer after four years. The Executive Engineer then gets promoted to P1 but is not considered as next rank. If the person of this rank wants to apply for his next promotion, he/she will have to undergo interview. If a person does well in interview, he/she is promoted to next and the final rank or else they'll remain as P1

Chief Engineer Promotion

After about 19years of being engineer and getting promoted, one then becomes the Chief Engineer which is the highest rank of Engineer.
